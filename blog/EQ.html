Emotional Intellegence

    What is it?



    You could view Emotional Intellegence (EQ) as:

    • How we interpret ourselves and others
    • Where our emotions arise from
    • How we may best navigate our fears and wishes

Difference between EQ and IQ and why EQ is relevant

Iq is an inborn ability and is measured upon implementing logical reasoning, problem solving and abstract thinking. EQ is a learned ability and is measured based on ABOVE

There is a direct correlation between those with high EQ and living a successful life both financially and in terms of relationships. It is extremely relevant in stressful, team-oriented careers such as programming due to the requirement of being able to manage and understand not only your own emotions but those around you.
